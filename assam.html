Famous Foods of Assam

Assamese Fish Tenga

Assamese Fish Tenga Recipe (English):

  • Ingredients:
    • 500g freshwater fish (like Rohu or Catla)
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 1 potato, cut into cubes
    • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 2 green chilies, slit
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed
  • Instructions:
    1. Marinate the fish pieces with turmeric and salt for 10 minutes.
    2. Heat mustard oil in a pan and fry the fish pieces until golden brown. Set aside.
    3. In the same pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
    4. Add potato cubes, turmeric powder, and salt. Sauté for a few minutes.
    5. Add water and bring it to a boil. Let it simmer until potatoes are cooked.
    6. Add fried fish pieces and lemon juice. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
    7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Khar

Khar Recipe (English):

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 raw papaya, peeled and chopped
    • 1 tablespoon alkali (made from dried banana peel)
    • 1 teaspoon mustard oil
    • 2 green chilies, slit
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed
  • Instructions:
    1. Heat mustard oil in a pot and add green chilies and turmeric powder. Sauté for a minute.
    2. Add chopped papaya and stir well. Cook for 5 minutes.
    3. Add water and alkali. Bring to a boil, then simmer until the papaya is tender.
    4. Adjust salt and cook for another few minutes until the flavors meld together.
    5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
