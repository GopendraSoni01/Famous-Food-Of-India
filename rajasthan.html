Dal Bati Churma

Dal Bati Churma Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • For Dal:
      • 1 cup split yellow moong dal
      • 1 onion, chopped
      • 2 tomatoes, chopped
      • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
      • Spices (cumin, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala)
      • Salt to taste
      • Fresh coriander for garnish
    • For Bati:
      • 2 cups whole wheat flour
      • 1/2 cup ghee
      • 1 teaspoon baking powder
      • Salt to taste
    • For Churma:
      • 1 cup wheat flour bati, crumbled
      • 1/2 cup ghee
      • 1/2 cup jaggery or sugar
      • Cardamom powder
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook the dal with water and spices until soft.
    2. For bati, mix flour, ghee, baking powder, and salt. Form into balls and bake until golden.
    3. For churma, crumble the baked batis, mix with ghee, jaggery, and cardamom powder.
    4. Serve the dal with batis and churma on the side, garnished with coriander.

Lal Maas

Lal Maas Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 500g mutton, cut into pieces
    • 4-5 dried red chilies
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, pureed
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
    • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Instructions:
    1. Soak dried red chilies in warm water for 15 minutes, then blend into a paste.
    2. Heat mustard oil in a pan and sauté cumin seeds and chopped onions until golden.
    3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
    4. Add the mutton pieces and cook until they are browned.
    5. Stir in the tomato puree, red chili paste, coriander powder, turmeric, and salt.
    6. Add water and simmer until the mutton is tender (about 45 minutes to 1 hour).
    7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or roti.

Gatte Ki Sabzi

Gatte Ki Sabzi Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • Salt to taste
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, pureed
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Instructions:
    1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin seeds, and salt. Add water to make a dough.
    2. Roll the dough into cylindrical shapes and steam for 15-20 minutes.
    3. Cut the steamed dough into small pieces.
    4. In a pan, heat oil and sauté onions until golden brown.
    5. Add tomato puree, cook until oil separates, then add yogurt and mix well.
    6. Add gatte pieces, garam masala, and cook for another 5 minutes.
    7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with roti or rice.
