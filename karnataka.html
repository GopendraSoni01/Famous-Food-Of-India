Bisi Bele Bath

Bisi Bele Bath Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup rice
    • 1/2 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
    • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
    • 1/4 cup tamarind pulp
    • 2 tablespoons Bisi Bele Bath powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • Curry leaves
    • 2 tablespoons ghee
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook rice and toor dal separately until soft.
    2. Heat ghee in a pan, add mustard seeds and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
    3. Add mixed vegetables and sauté until slightly tender.
    4. Add tamarind pulp, Bisi Bele Bath powder, and salt. Cook for 5 minutes.
    5. Mix in cooked rice and dal. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    6. Serve hot, garnished with coriander leaves and a dollop of ghee.

Mysore Pak

Mysore Pak Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
    • 2 cups sugar
    • 1 cup ghee
    • 1 cup water
    • A pinch of cardamom powder
  • Instructions:
    1. Roast gram flour in ghee until golden brown. Set aside.
    2. In a pan, boil sugar and water until a thick syrup forms.
    3. Gradually add roasted gram flour to the syrup, stirring continuously.
    4. Keep adding ghee while stirring until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
    5. Pour the mixture into a greased tray, spread evenly, and allow it to set.
    6. Cut into squares and serve once cooled.

Neer Dosa

Neer Dosa Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup rice
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed
  • Instructions:
    1. Soak rice in water for 4-5 hours. Drain and grind to a fine batter with water.
    2. Add salt and additional water to achieve a thin, watery consistency.
    3. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladleful of batter, spreading evenly.
    4. Cover and cook for a minute without flipping.
    5. Remove dosa from the pan and repeat with remaining batter.
    6. Serve hot with coconut chutney or sambar.
