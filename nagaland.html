Smoked Pork with Bamboo Shoot

Smoked Pork with Bamboo Shoot Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 500g smoked pork
    • 100g bamboo shoots
    • 2 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped
    • 2 green chilies, slit
    • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Cut the smoked pork into bite-sized pieces.
    2. Boil the pork in water for about 20 minutes to reduce the fat content.
    3. Drain the water and set the pork aside.
    4. In a pan, add the pork, bamboo shoots, tomatoes, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste.
    5. Cook on medium heat, adding water as needed, until the pork becomes tender.
    6. Adjust the salt and cook until the bamboo shoots are soft.
    7. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Galho (Naga Porridge)

Galho Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup rice
    • 100g smoked meat (optional)
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (potatoes, beans, etc.)
    • 1 tsp ginger, minced
    • 2 garlic cloves, minced
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Wash the rice and soak it for 10 minutes.
    2. In a pot, add rice, smoked meat, and enough water to cook the rice.
    3. Add minced ginger, garlic, and salt to taste.
    4. Bring it to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer.
    5. Add mixed vegetables and cook until the rice is soft and porridge-like.
    6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.

Axone (Fermented Soybean) with Pork

Axone with Pork Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 500g pork, cut into pieces
    • 3 tbsp Axone (fermented soybean)
    • 2-3 dried red chilies
    • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. In a pot, add the pork pieces and enough water to cover them.
    2. Bring to a boil and cook until the pork is tender.
    3. Drain the water and set aside the pork.
    4. In a pan, dry roast the dried red chilies and Axone.
    5. Grind the roasted chilies and Axone to make a coarse paste.
    6. Add the paste to the pork, along with ginger-garlic paste, and cook for about 10 minutes.
    7. Add salt to taste and cook until everything is well combined.
    8. Serve hot with rice.
