Famous Foods of Madhya Pradesh

Poha

Poha Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 cups poha (flattened rice)
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 1 green chili, chopped
    • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 cup peanuts
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • Coriander leaves for garnish
    • Lemon juice to taste
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Rinse poha in water and drain. Set aside.
    2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and peanuts. Sauté until peanuts are roasted.
    3. Add chopped onions and green chili. Sauté until onions are translucent.
    4. Add turmeric powder and salt. Stir well.
    5. Add poha and mix thoroughly. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
    6. Garnish with coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon juice.
    7. Serve hot.

Bhutte ka Kees

Bhutte ka Kees Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 cups grated corn
    • 1 cup milk
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1 green chili, chopped
    • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 tablespoon ghee
    • Coriander leaves for garnish
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Heat ghee in a pan, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Sauté until they crackle.
    2. Add onions and green chili. Sauté until onions are translucent.
    3. Add turmeric powder and grated corn. Mix well.
    4. Add milk and cook until the corn is tender and the mixture thickens.
    5. Garnish with coriander leaves.
    6. Serve hot with roti or rice.

Dal Bafla

Dal Bafla Recipe:

  • Dal Ingredients:
    • 1 cup arhar dal (pigeon pea)
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for cooking
  • Bafla Ingredients:
    • 2 cups wheat flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon caraway seeds (ajwain)
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Water for kneading
  • Instructions:
    1. For Dal: Cook arhar dal with turmeric powder until soft. Prepare a tempering with cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, and onions, then add tomatoes and spices. Mix with cooked dal and simmer.
    2. For Bafla: Prepare a dough with wheat flour, caraway seeds, cumin seeds, turmeric, and salt. Shape into round balls and cook them in boiling water. Once cooked, bake or roast until golden.
    3. Serve dal with bafla, topped with ghee if desired.
