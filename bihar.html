Famous Foods of Bihar

Litti Chokha

Litti Chokha Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 cups wheat flour
    • 1 cup sattu (roasted gram flour)
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon mustard oil
    • Spices (ajwain, kalonji, etc.)
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Mix the wheat flour with water and knead into a dough.
    2. Mix sattu with spices, onions, and mustard oil for stuffing.
    3. Make small balls of dough, stuff with sattu mix, and shape into littis.
    4. Bake the littis in a tandoor or on a grill until golden brown.
    5. Serve hot with chokha made of mashed vegetables.

Sattu Paratha

Sattu Paratha Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 2 cups wheat flour
    • 1 cup sattu
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 green chilies, chopped
    • Salt and spices as needed
  • Instructions:
    1. Knead the wheat flour with water to form dough.
    2. Prepare a stuffing mix with sattu, onions, chilies, and spices.
    3. Divide the dough into balls, stuff with the sattu mix, and roll into parathas.
    4. Cook the parathas on a hot tawa with ghee until golden brown.
    5. Serve with pickles and yogurt.

Thekua

Thekua Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup wheat flour
    • 1/2 cup jaggery
    • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
    • 1 tablespoon ghee
    • Cardamom powder
  • Instructions:
    1. Mix wheat flour, jaggery, coconut, ghee, and cardamom powder.
    2. Form a dough by adding water as needed.
    3. Shape into small discs and deep fry until golden brown.
    4. Cool and store in an airtight container.
    5. Serve as a snack with tea or coffee.
