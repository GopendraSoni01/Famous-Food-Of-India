Puttu and Kadala Curry

Puttu and Kadala Curry Recipe:

  • Puttu Ingredients:
    • 2 cups rice flour
    • 1 cup grated coconut
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • Water as needed
  • Kadala Curry Ingredients:
    • 1 cup black chickpeas (kadala)
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 2 tablespoons coconut, grated
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for cooking
  • Instructions:
    1. For Puttu: Mix rice flour with salt and enough water to form a crumbly mixture. Layer the mixture and grated coconut in a puttu maker. Steam for 10 minutes.
    2. For Kadala Curry: Soak black chickpeas overnight and cook until tender. Heat oil, add onions, and sauté until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
    3. Add spices and cooked chickpeas, simmer for 10 minutes. Grind grated coconut and add to the curry. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
    4. Serve hot with Puttu.

Appam with Stew

Appam with Stew Recipe:

  • Appam Ingredients:
    • 1 cup rice
    • 1/4 cup grated coconut
    • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
    • 1/2 teaspoon yeast
    • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed
  • Stew Ingredients:
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, potato, beans)
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1 green chili, slit
    • 1/2 cup coconut milk
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for cooking
  • Instructions:
    1. For Appam: Soak rice and urad dal overnight. Grind with grated coconut, yeast, and sugar into a smooth batter. Let it ferment for 8 hours.
    2. Heat a non-stick appam pan and pour a ladle of batter. Swirl to cover the pan. Cook until the edges are crispy and the center is soft.
    3. For Stew: Heat oil, add onions, and sauté until soft. Add ginger-garlic paste, vegetables, and spices. Add coconut milk and cook until vegetables are tender.
    4. Serve Appam with hot stew.

Kerala Sadya

Kerala Sadya Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • Rice
    • Parippu (yellow dal)
    • Avial (mixed vegetable curry)
    • Thoran (vegetable stir-fry)
    • Olan (pumpkin and black-eyed pea curry)
    • Pickle
    • Curd
    • Chips
    • Banana
    • Papadam
  • Instructions:
    1. Prepare each dish separately as per traditional recipes.
    2. Serve rice with a variety of curries and side dishes on a banana leaf.
    3. Enjoy the meal as a complete Sadya with all accompaniments.
