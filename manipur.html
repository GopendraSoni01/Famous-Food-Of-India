Famous Foods of Manipur

Eromba

Eromba Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 4-5 green chilies
    • 2 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 1 tablespoon fermented fish (optional)
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Instructions:
    1. Heat oil in a pan, add onions and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until onions turn golden brown.
    2. Add green chilies and turmeric powder. Cook for a minute.
    3. Add boiled potatoes and mix well. Cook for a few minutes, adding water as needed to adjust consistency.
    4. Add fermented fish if using, and salt. Cook for another 5 minutes.
    5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice.

Chakhao Kheer

Chakhao Kheer Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup Chakhao rice (black rice)
    • 2 cups milk
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 1/4 cup dried fruits (cashews, almonds, raisins)
    • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
    • 1 tablespoon ghee
    • Water as needed
  • Instructions:
    1. Wash and soak Chakhao rice in water for 30 minutes.
    2. Heat ghee in a pan, add dried fruits, and sauté until golden brown.
    3. Add soaked rice and cook for a few minutes.
    4. Add milk and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and the mixture thickens.
    5. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.
    6. Serve warm or chilled.

Singju

Singju Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup kangkong (water spinach) or any leafy greens
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon roasted sesame seeds
    • 1 tablespoon fermented bamboo shoots (optional)
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Heat oil in a pan, add onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions are translucent.
    2. Add leafy greens and cook until wilted.
    3. Add roasted sesame seeds and fermented bamboo shoots if using.
    4. Mix well and cook for a few more minutes.
    5. Season with salt and serve warm with steamed rice.
