Vada Pav

Vada Pav Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 4-6 pav (bread rolls)
    • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
    • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
    • Water to make batter
    • Oil for frying
  • Instructions:
    1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and turmeric powder.
    2. Add mashed potatoes and red chili powder. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Allow to cool.
    3. Form potato mixture into balls.
    4. Prepare a batter with besan and water. Dip potato balls in the batter and fry until golden brown.
    5. Assemble Vada Pav by placing a vada (potato ball) inside each pav. Serve with chutneys and fried green chilies.

Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 4-6 pav (bread rolls)
    • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, carrots, peas, etc.), boiled and mashed
    • 1 large onion, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 teaspoons pav bhaji masala
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 tablespoon butter
    • Salt to taste
    • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Instructions:
    1. Heat butter in a pan, add onions and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until onions are golden brown.
    2. Add tomatoes, pav bhaji masala, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
    3. Add mashed vegetables and mix well. Cook for a few minutes, adding water as needed to adjust consistency.
    4. Garnish with coriander leaves and a dollop of butter.
    5. Serve hot with buttered pav (bread rolls) toasted on a griddle.

Puran Poli

Puran Poli Recipe:

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
    • 1 cup jaggery, grated
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
    • 1 tablespoon ghee
    • Water as needed
    • Salt to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook chana dal in water until soft. Drain and mash.
    2. Mix mashed dal with jaggery, turmeric powder, and cardamom powder. Cook until thickened and allow to cool.
    3. Prepare a dough with all-purpose flour, ghee, and water. Divide into small balls.
    4. Flatten each dough ball and fill with the dal mixture. Roll out into thin circles.
    5. Cook each poli on a hot griddle until golden brown on both sides.
    6. Serve warm with ghee or milk.
